# FairPay Explanation (For Judges)

## The Story
Sarah is a freelance designer. She just spent 3 weeks meticulously drafting a brand package. She sends the final ZIP, and the client goes completely silent. The standard platform (Upwork) demands a 20% cut for the privilege of mediation, which is a stressful, centralized, and subjective process. She has no real recourse.

## The Solution
FairPay solves Sarah’s problem at the root. Before she draws a single stroke, the client's money is locked in a smart contract. The contract is the objective rule engine. It recognizes programmable, verifiable milestones.

When Sarah submits her files via IPFS (or pushes her code to GitHub), an immutable 48-hour window begins.
If the client ignores her? **The funds release automatically.**
If the client hates it? **They must stake a financial bond to raise a dispute, eliminating casual abuse.**
If the client likes 80% of it, but wants a revision on 20%? **They can dispute exactly 20%, letting the 80% release to Sarah so she can pay rent.**

Blockchain isn't just a database here — it provides programmable money, absolute immutability, and zero middlemen.

## The 12 Features
1. **Multi-milestone escrow contract**: Lock up 100%, release in chunks.
2. **Three verification methods**: Signatures, GitHub hashes, IPFS hashes.
3. **Dispute window + default release**: Prevent indefinite stalling. 
4. **Staked dispute resolution**: 3-of-3 multi-sig.
5. **Dashboard**: Complete visibility. 
6. **Partial dispute**: Not an all-or-nothing nuclear option. Rare in any escrow platform.
7. **Soulbound Reputation NFTs**: A resume that nobody can fake and platforms cannot seize.
8. **GitHub webhook auto-release**: Complete developer automation.
9. **Retainer mode**: Auto-renewing smart escrows that cancel linearly.
10. **On-chain templates**: "Web Dev Sprint" is a pre-packaged smart contract.
11. **Skill-staked arbitration**: Mediators bet their own reputation on fairness.
12. **Public job board**: Bidding costs a small bond, filtering out spam applicants.

## The Impact
The global freelance market is $1.5 Trillion. Over 85% of freelancers report experiencing uncompensated work or drastically late payments. Platform cuts bleed billions from creators every year.

By moving the logic to the EVM and automating dispute tracking with absolute cryptoeconomic disincentives, FairPay replaces trust with math, completely upending the incentive models that legacy networks rely on.
